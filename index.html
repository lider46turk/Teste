Controlled intelligence. WhiteModder // origin.log GAZ TEAM started as a collective of modders inside the SWTeam group. The journey began with Free Fire — our first testing ground. While exploring new possibilities, we discovered Simple Sandbox 2. At that point, our path diverged. We separated from SWTeam and continued independently. GAZ TEAM was formed. From 2021 to 2026, we focused on modding, experimenting, and learning. Slow progress. Real experience. Project by project, the group evolved. Today, GAZ TEAM operates with over 300 active members. And this is not the final build. We are expanding. More games. More mods. More control. Stay tuned.
